Technology Learning at TEKLRN

Delivers Software, IT Technology Training, Video Courses, Options to Book Trainer

Technology Training Content

Teklrn maintains the latest, up-to-date data, on every technology training content it delivers. With our staff working round to the clock to update every content with latest release of software and removing obsolete data. This way we ensure our software technology training courses provide the highest value to the learners/customers.

Video and Live Trainer Sessions

Software/IT technology training courses are offered in the form video training content and with the option to book trainer for one-to-one live sessions. Most technology training content has a set of free training video content, that explains the initial basics of the programming language / technology and set up on operating system.

Teklrns approach to training

Software/Programming languages are divided into levels. All the video courses are based on the syllabus for that level, the topics are explained by running the software in a live Operating System (Windows/UNIX) and server environment. Syllabus for each level is freely accessible on our website. That aids deciding the level to book.
