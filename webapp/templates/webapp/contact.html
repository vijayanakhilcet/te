TEKLRN approach

An on demand learning experience with complete control to the learner on what they want to learn.

Technology Trends 2020

Technology industry is more volatile than ever. With new releases of existing technologies and new technologies arising to solve complex business problems

Certified Professional Trainers

Teklrn Courses are booked per Level with a timing of choice, Our qualified Trainers pick up the booking and the Trainee is notified when trainer is assigned to the Booking

Our approach to training

We divide technology into multiple levels. The Trainee has the choice to get trained in level of choice. Syllabus for each level is freely accessible on our website. That aids deciding the level to book

Python

The Python 2 language, i.e. Python 2.7.x, was officially discontinued on January 1, 2020 (first planned for 2015) after which security patches and other improvements will not be released for it. With Python 2's end-of-life, only Python 3.5.x and later are supported. Python interpreters are available for many operating systems.

Whom our courses benefit

Beginners with no programming experience (Recommended approach is to start from Level 1 of the Technology). Experienced Professional who want to Learn New technology (Recommended approach is to start with Level 1 and reach the Level required for their need. Its adviced to read the Syllabus of each level to decide what to learn). Highly experienced professionals who want to learn higher level of the technology they already know(Recommended approach is to check the levels and figure out the level they want to start learning and book the courses)

Instructions for Trainees

Click the button "Get Started" on the Home Page. Enter the technology on the search box and select the option from the list. Select the level on the left panel of the screen to view the syllabus. To book a level for training click "Book Course" button.
